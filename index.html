Maintenance Tips

Flush sediment annually and test the T&P valve. Typical lifespan is 8–12 years depending on water quality and maintenance.

Insulate hose bibbs, disconnect hoses, and drip faucets on exterior walls during hard freezes. Know where your main shutoff is located.
